Help Center

Welcome to the Help Center! Here, you'll find answers to common questions about using our Invoice Generator application.

How do I create a new invoice?

To create a new invoice, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to the "Invoices" section.
  3. Click the "New Invoice" button.
  4. Fill in the required details such as client information, products, and due dates.
  5. Save the invoice and it will be added to your list of invoices.

How can I customize invoice templates?

You can customize invoice templates to match your brand's identity. Here's how:

  1. Go to the "Settings" section of your account.
  2. Choose a template that you want to customize.
  3. Modify the layout, colors, and logo to reflect your brand's visual style.
  4. Save your changes to update the template.

Why are my invoices not displaying correctly?

If you're experiencing issues with invoice display, try the following:

  • Check your internet connection and refresh the page.
  • Clear your browser's cache and cookies.
  • Ensure that your browser is updated to the latest version.

How can I download my invoices as PDF files?

To download your invoices as PDF files, follow these steps:

  1. Open the invoice you want to download.
  2. Click the "Download as PDF" button.
  3. Your browser will prompt you to save the PDF file to your device.
