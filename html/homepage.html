Faq-Item

How Does It Work?

Sending e-invoices through the network is an easy process:

  • 1.Create a free Invoiced account.
  • 2.Invite your buyer to join your Invoiced network.
  • 3.Once connected to your buyer on the network, you can send invoices from Invoiced.

How Do I Get Paid?

After signing up, you can enable the payment methods you want to accept in your Invoiced account. Buyers can pay with direct debit, virtual card, and credit card.

How Do I Access My Invoices?

Invoices are securely stored in the cloud in case you ever need to retrieve them for tax or legal reasons.

How Much Does It Cost?

Invoiced is 100% free to send and receive invoices. If you choose to accept payments through the network, there is a payment processing fee when your buyer makes a successful payment.

Why Is It Better Than Email?

With e-invoicing, your invoices no longer get lost in someone's email inbox. In addition to sending a PDF of the invoice, your buyer will receive line item level invoice data that can be plugged into their accounts payable portal or accounting system.

How Secure Is the Network?

Sending invoices through Invoiced is a more secure option than sending invoices through email. E-invoicing is also beneficial to your buyers, as they will be assured when they receive invoices from you through the network that they are authentic.
